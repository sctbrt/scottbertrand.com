Recommendations

Best Overall: Option A (Warm Amber) — #D97706

Balances warmth with professionalism. Reads as architectural/premium without being too aggressive. Good contrast in both light and dark modes.

Runner-up: Option D (Deep Terracotta) — #B45309

More muted and sophisticated. Feels earthy and grounded. May work better if you want less visual pop.

Avoid: Option C (Soft Coral) — #F97316

Too bright and playful for the architectural/premium feel of the site. Could work for a different brand positioning.

Suggested Application (Conservative Approach)

  • Section headers (H2) — accent color text
  • Step numbers — accent color with subtle background glow
  • Accent bars above key sections — thin 2-3px line
  • Keep primary CTAs dark/light (current style)
  • Keep glass panels neutral (no orange glow)
